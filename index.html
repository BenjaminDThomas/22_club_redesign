Facilities

  • From hand-pulled ales to cocktails, our fully stocked bar is the perfect place to relax between frames. Whether you're celebrating a win or just unwinding, the bar team are on hand to look after you.

  • We have full-size professional snooker tables available for members. Whether you're a seasoned player or just learning the game, our tables are maintained to the highest standard for a great playing experience.

  • Enjoy both American and English pool at 01 Club. Our pool tables are perfect for casual games with friends or more competitive play. Tables are available throughout opening hours for all members.

  • Test your aim on our dedicated darts boards. A great way to round off a session or challenge your mates to a friendly match. Available to all members during opening hours.
