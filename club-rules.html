Club Rules

Please take note of the club rules before planning your trip, thank you.

  1. No under 14's at any time.
  2. No under 18's after 7pm.
  3. No food or drink to be brought into the club.
  4. No more than 6 to a table.
  5. No smoking or vaping.
  6. No sitting or putting drinks on the play tables.
  7. We have a zero tolerance policy to drug and or threatening behaviour.

The bar staffs word is final.
