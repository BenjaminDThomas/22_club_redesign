01 Club

Eligibility

To use the tables (Snooker, American & English pool) at the 22 Club you are required to have a membership. You will need to bring a valid photo ID with you to become a member (Driving Licence or Passport).

Membership Costs

Membership lasts 12 Months

Full Membership£14.95
Student Membership Valid student card to be shown to a member of staff £9.95
14 yrs to 17 yrs Membership Valid from noon till 7pm £7
OAP Membership£5.50
